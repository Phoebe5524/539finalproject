The Single Spark.


The Planning and Design of Yuyao Ceramic Kiln


Individual Work


Duration:March-June, 2021


Nowadays, although the scale of Jingdezhen's ceramic industry is expandingconstantly, the proportion of hand-made art ceramics is still very low, and the number of individual business of hand-made ceramics is decreasing. So the skills and culture of hand-made ceramics are facing the fault of inheritance. With the consideration of production conditions of Yuyao Ceramic Kiln, natural conditions and current layout, in order to arouse the awareness of the importance and protection of hand-made ceramic technology, several design strategies were put forward to help create an immersive hand-made ceramic technology and cultural interaction environment.

Multiverse——The Moon and Sixpence.


UIA-HYP Cup 2020 International Student Competition in Architecture Design


Group Work


My contribution: Diagram&Analysis Part of the investigation&Concept Design&Model Landscape Architecture Part Design of Overall Style

Duration:July-September, 2020


The ancient town of Yangliuqing, located on the outskirts of Tianjin, has a complex population composition, as do the sprawling fringes of many big cities. The migrants from the villages, towns and metropolis converge here, forming a rich and diverse life style and cultural background. Both urban and rural style will coexist here. One the one hand, the prosperous but crowded city and the picturesque but poor countryside are the memories and pursuits of different groups of people, the moon here and the sixpence there; on the other hand, they can make up for the hidden loss of farming area while realizing the modernization of life style.

The Twisted Rubik's Cube.


Student Centre Design


Individual Work


Duration:April-December, 2020


The project is a new student centre located in Nanjing Forestry University. The new student spaces were intended to relieve pressure from overcrowding in the school library, stimulate the creativity of students, and work to create a larger student social and study precinct at the heart of the campus by connecting students with one another in a dynamic and inspiring setting.


With the reserved pond in the central of the site, the atrium space is designed for connecting the students to the nature. The stepped roof garden gives students access to the second floor. The functions include sports, community activities and study.

The Biological-breath Corner.


Wetland Park Design


Individual Work


Duration:September, 2019- February, 2020


The site is located on the side of Yangtze River flowing through Nanjing, and is a rare riverside beach oasis in high density city.

This project is intended to be a site for nature education, bird watching, fishing, hiking, and quiet contemplation by keeping the tidal flat wetland environment as far as possible, setting up pedestrian corridor, and increasing the wetland experience space for citizens; and hundreds of wildlife species populate here.Using the self-purification capacity of water body and ecological filtration of greenland, the water can finally become safe and accessible to the public. At the same time, with wetlands and different types of revetments, the problems of waterlogging in rainy season and drought in dry season can be solved, and the average allocation of water can be realized.
